# 🪙 Loan Eligibility Prediction

## 1 Executive Summary
A financial institution is seeking digital transformation in its loan services by automating the decision-making process.the project aims to build a predictive classification model. This model will analyze customer attributes (such as credit history, income, and loan amount) the moment they are submitted via the online application. This will result in real-time identification of customer eligibility, allowing the company to efficiently target profitable segments, reduce manual workload, and significantly improve response times for loan applications.


## 2 Client
* **Financial Institution / Bank:** The specific name of the company is not mentioned, but it is an organization that provides loans to customers online.

## 3 Problem
* **Manual and Time-Consuming Processes:** Evaluating loan applications and determining customer eligibility manually requires significant time and effort. The company needs a system that can evaluate these applications in real-time as soon as the customer inputs their details (e.g., income, marital status, credit history, etc.) into the online form.

## 4 Business Objective
* **Automate Loan Decisions:** Make the approval or rejection process instantaneous and automated.
* **Targeted Marketing:** Identify eligible customer segments to specifically target them in marketing campaigns, maximizing conversions and eliminating the effort wasted on non-eligible segments.

## 5 Data Mining Goal
* **Build a Classification Model:** Utilize the provided dataset (615 rows and 13 variables) to train an algorithm capable of classifying new customers into two categories (Eligible / Not Eligible for a loan) based on their demographic and financial attributes.


## 6 Success Criteria & Expected Business Benefits

### Success Criteria
* **Technical:** Achieve high accuracy and precision in the classification model. Specifically, minimize "False Positives" to avoid granting loans to customers who are likely to default.
* **Operational:** Successfully integrate the model into the company's online platform to process inputs and return decisions efficiently in real-time without system latency.

### Expected Business Benefits
* **Reduce Operational Costs:** Decrease human intervention and the time spent manually reviewing individual loan applications.
* **Enhance Customer Experience (UX):** Provide customers with instant feedback on their application status, greatly improving customer satisfaction.
* **Increase Revenue & Mitigate Risk:** Accurately direct marketing efforts and loan approvals toward customers with high creditworthiness (lower risk), thereby increasing the volume of successfully issued and repaid loans.
